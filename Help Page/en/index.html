App Introduction

Repz is a comprehensive application for managing and tracking your gym workouts. Whether you're a beginner or a professional, the app helps you achieve your fitness goals by providing ready-made training programs or creating your own custom program.

Getting Started

1

Language Selection

When you first open the app, you'll be asked to choose your preferred language (Arabic or English).

2

Training Program Type

Choose between a ready-made training program or creating your own custom training program.

3

Ready Programs

If you choose a ready program, you'll find options like high intensity low volume, low intensity high volume, etc.

Ready Training Programs

The app offers a variety of training programs prepared by fitness experts:

Bro Split

Advanced training program for muscle splitting over 5 days a week.

Arnold

Powerful program inspired by bodybuilding legend Arnold Schwarzenegger.

Full Body

Complete body workouts suitable for beginners and intermediates.

Upper Lower

Program focusing on splitting exercises into upper and lower days.

Push Pull Legs

Program focusing on splitting muscles by function (push, pull, then legs).

Note: Ab and forearm exercises are available for free to all users.

Purchasing Training Programs

To purchase any training program:

1

Choose Intensity

Select the intensity level that suits you (high, low).

2

Choose Program

Select the program you want to purchase from the list.

3

Press Purchase Button

A page will appear showing the program price and a purchase button.

4

Enter Serial

Enter the serial number you get from customer service.

5

Activated

After successfully entering the serial, the purchase button will change to a program display button.

Warning: Each serial works only once and on one device only. If you delete and reinstall the app, you will lose the serial.

Recording Exercises and Progress

The app gives you advanced tools to record and track your exercise progress:

Recording Weights and Reps

Record the weight used, number of reps, and sets for each exercise.

Data Saving

Your data is automatically saved and you can refer to it at any time.

View History

Press on the exercise image to view all previous records with date and time.

View History

The last workout is displayed under each exercise.

Reset

The reset button clears current data while keeping old records.

Custom Training Program

If you choose to create your own custom training program:

1

Enter Serial

You'll need a serial to activate the custom program feature.

2

Design Your Program

Specify the number of days, exercises per day, and number of sets.

3

Start Training

After finishing the design, the program will be created and you can start immediately.

4

Serial Renewal

You can renew the serial before its expiration date to avoid service interruption.

Note: The custom program serial is available for different periods (week, month, 3 months, 6 months, year, permanent).

You can renew the serial a day or more before its expiration to avoid any interruption in using the program.

Mandatory Updates

Sometimes we release mandatory updates to improve the app and add new features. When a mandatory update is released:

1

Update Notification

You'll receive a notification in the app informing you of the need to update to continue usage.

2

Mandatory Update

You won't be able to use the app until you install the new update.

3

Data Preservation

Your data and records are safely preserved and won't be lost after the update.

Note: We always recommend keeping the app updated for the best user experience and highest performance.

App Navigation

Back Button

Home Button

Returns you to the home page to change settings or program.
